About Our College

Malla Reddy College of Teacher Education (B.Ed & M.Ed) is approved by NCTE and affiliated to Osmania University. Teachers are to be considered as nation builders. The New Education Policy, 1986 has in unequivocal terms reiterated the need for an effective system of Teacher Education. Recognizing the growing necessity and increasing importance of quality Teacher Education to meet the challenges of 21st century

Malla Reddy College of Teacher Education recognizes that the faculty forms a pivotal part of any educational institution and believes that the rich academic and research credentials and dedication of the faculty are the major building blocks of any reputed educational institution. Hence, it has appointed highly experienced and well qualified faculty with proven abilities and accomplishments.

OUR CHAIRMAN :- The chairman of the society is Shri Ch.Mahender Reddy & the founder of the society is Shri CH. Malla Reddy is a great educationalist and philanthropist with a vision. He has been in the field of education for the last 20 years imparting quality education to the children in the backward localities.
