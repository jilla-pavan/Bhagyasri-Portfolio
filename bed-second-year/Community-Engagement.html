Community Engagement

Community Engagement involves actively participating in community-based activities to understand social issues, build meaningful relationships, and contribute to the well-being of society. It provides opportunities to interact with community members, identify local needs, and support initiatives that promote education, health, environmental awareness, and social development. Through community engagement, individuals develop empathy, leadership, teamwork, and communication skills while fostering a sense of social responsibility. This experience helps bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world challenges, encouraging active citizenship and community participation.
