Health & Well-being

Health and Well-being focuses on promoting physical, mental, emotional, and social wellness for a balanced and healthy lifestyle. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining good health through proper nutrition, regular physical activity, stress management, and positive relationships. This area helps individuals develop healthy habits, improve self-awareness, and enhance their overall quality of life. By understanding the significance of well-being, individuals can make informed choices that support personal growth, productivity, and long-term health.
