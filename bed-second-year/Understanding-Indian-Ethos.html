Understanding Indian Ethos

Understanding Indian Ethos and Knowledge Systems involves exploring the rich cultural, philosophical, and intellectual heritage of India that has influenced its development through the ages. Indian ethos focuses on values such as duty, harmony, respect for diversity, and balanced living, while Indian knowledge systems encompass traditional sciences, arts, educational practices, and spiritual teachings. Together, they emphasize the importance of combining ethical values with intellectual growth and sustainable development. In teacher education, this understanding enables future educators to integrate traditional wisdom with contemporary teaching practices, promoting culturally aware and meaningful learning experiences.
