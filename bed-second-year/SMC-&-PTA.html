SMC & PTA

School Management Committee (SMC) and Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) play a vital role in promoting cooperation between schools, parents, teachers, and the community. School Management Committee (SMC): Established under the Right to Education Act, SMCs consist of parents, teachers, and community members who work together to support school development. They help monitor school activities, improve educational quality, and ensure effective school management. Parent-Teacher Association (PTA): PTAs serve as a platform for parents and teachers to communicate and collaborate on matters related to students’ academic progress and overall development. They encourage parental participation, strengthen school-community relationships, and contribute to a positive learning environment.
