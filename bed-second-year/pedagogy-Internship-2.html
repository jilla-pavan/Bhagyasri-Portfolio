Pedagogy Internship-2

Pedagogy – second Practicum is the concluding phase of teacher education that provides student-teachers with the opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills in real classroom settings. During this practicum, they plan and deliver lessons, manage classroom activities, address the needs of diverse learners, and reflect on their teaching experiences under guidance and supervision. It focuses on the practical application of pedagogical principles, bridging the gap between theory and practice, and strengthening professional competence. By the end of the practicum, student-teachers are expected to demonstrate effective teaching, classroom management, communication, and student assessment skills.
