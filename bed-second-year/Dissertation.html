Dissertation

A dissertation is an independent research project undertaken to explore a specific topic in depth. It involves identifying a research problem, reviewing relevant literature, collecting and analyzing data, and presenting findings in a systematic manner. Through the dissertation process, students develop critical thinking, research, analytical, and problem-solving skills while gaining a deeper understanding of their chosen area of study. It provides an opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge to practical research and contributes to academic and professional development.
