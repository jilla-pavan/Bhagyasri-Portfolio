Internship Final-Practicum

The Internship Final–Practicum is a comprehensive school-based training program that provides teacher trainees with hands-on teaching experience in a real classroom environment. It involves planning and delivering lessons, managing classrooms, assessing student performance, and participating in various school activities. This practicum enables trainees to apply theoretical knowledge to practical teaching situations, develop professional competencies, and enhance their instructional and communication skills. It also helps build confidence, responsibility, and a deeper understanding of the roles and responsibilities of a teacher, preparing them for future careers in education
