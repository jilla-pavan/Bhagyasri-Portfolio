Citizenship education, Sustainability

Citizenship Education, Sustainability Education, and Environment Education are important components of teacher education that help develop responsible and informed individuals. Citizenship Education promotes an understanding of rights, responsibilities, democratic principles, and active involvement in society. It encourages values such as respect, cooperation, and social responsibility. Sustainability Education focuses on the responsible use of resources, social equity, and practices that support the well-being of present and future generations. Environment Education creates awareness about environmental issues, ecological balance, climate change, and biodiversity conservation. It motivates learners to adopt environmentally friendly practices and contribute to the protection of natural resources.
