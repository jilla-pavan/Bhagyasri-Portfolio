Art In Education

Integrated art-based activities to support creativity and effective learning.

What Is Art In Education?

Art in Education involves incorporating creative activities into the teaching and learning process to make education more interactive and meaningful. It encourages creativity, critical thinking, and emotional development while increasing student engagement. Activities such as drawing, music, drama, and storytelling help learners express themselves and connect concepts with real-life experiences. In teacher education, art serves as an effective teaching tool that supports holistic development and promotes innovative classroom practices.
