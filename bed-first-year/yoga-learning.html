What Is Yoga Learning?

Yoga learning is a holistic educational activity that combines movement, breathing, awareness, concentration, and relaxation practices. Yoga supports physical fitness, emotional well-being, discipline, flexibility, and mental focus. For teachers and students, yoga promotes healthier habits and creates a positive learning environment.
