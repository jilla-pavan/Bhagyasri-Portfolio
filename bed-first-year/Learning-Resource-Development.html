What Is Learning Resource Development?

Learning Resources Development is the process of creating, organizing, and using educational materials to support effective teaching and learning. These resources may include textbooks, worksheets, charts, models, digital content, and other instructional tools. The purpose is to enhance student engagement, improve understanding, and support curriculum goals. In teacher education, learning resource development helps future teachers design and adapt materials to meet the diverse needs of learners while promoting creativity and inclusive teaching practices.
