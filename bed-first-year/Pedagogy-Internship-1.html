Pedagogy Internship-1

Pedagogy Final Practicum is the final phase of teacher education in which student-teachers put their knowledge and teaching skills into practice in real classroom environments. It includes lesson planning, classroom instruction, student assessment, and classroom management under the guidance of experienced mentors. This experience enables trainees to strengthen their teaching abilities, develop confidence, and improve their instructional techniques. The practicum acts as a vital link between academic learning and professional teaching, preparing future educators for successful classroom careers.
