What Is Peer Learning?

Peer learning is an educational approach where students learn from and with one another by sharing ideas, discussing concepts, teaching classmates, and participating collaboratively. This method develops communication skills, leadership, confidence, teamwork, and active participation. Peer teaching allows future educators to gain classroom experience while helping others learn.
